- **UpdateAssetSchema aliased to CreateAssetSchema** — all fields required on update (matching existing ledger patterns)
- **Duplicate name returns 409** — catches Postgres unique constraint violation in error handler
- **Asset delete is a hard delete** with no cascade effects (assets are standalone, not referenced by other tables)
